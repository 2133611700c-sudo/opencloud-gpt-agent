# Call Rescue Kit

Call Rescue Kit is a productized implementation service for small U.S. plumbing and HVAC businesses that lose inbound leads after hours or when calls are missed.

## Offer

- Missed-call text-back
- Narrow after-hours AI intake
- Lead qualification
- Callback or appointment routing
- Daily lead summary
- 14-day launch and tuning support

## Commercial model

Commodity AI receptionist subscriptions are inexpensive. Call Rescue Kit is therefore not sold as software access. The client pays for discovery, implementation, integration, guardrails, testing, reporting, and ongoing optimization.

- Founding-client package: $1,500 for implementation and the first 30 days
- Ongoing monitoring and tuning: $299/month, optional after month one
- Telephony and AI usage: billed separately at cost or provisioned directly in the client's account

Four founding-client packages produce $6,000 gross revenue. With third-party usage passed through and initial validation spending capped at $100, this creates room for approximately $5,000 in operating profit before taxes and payment-processing costs. It is a target, not a guarantee.

## Initial $100 budget

- Domain: up to $15
- AI phone-agent test balance: up to $30
- Domain email and deliverability: up to $20
- Hosting: use the free tier during validation
- Contingency: at least $35

No paid advertising is authorized during initial validation. Client-specific usage is activated only after payment or inside the client's own vendor account.

## Operating boundary

The agent system may research public business information, build evidence-based audits, prepare personalized outreach, create demos, and generate implementation assets. Sending outreach, purchasing services, charging customers, changing customer systems, or publishing publicly requires explicit human approval.

## Deployment

This directory is a static Vercel-compatible site. Create a new Vercel project from the repository and set the root directory to `products/call-rescue-kit`.

## 30-day success gates

1. Landing page deployed and independently audited.
2. 100 qualified plumbing/HVAC prospects with evidence of weak after-hours or missed-call handling.
3. 60 individually reviewed outreach drafts.
4. At least 8 qualified discovery calls.
5. Four paid founding-client implementations.

## Kill and pivot rules

- Fewer than three qualified replies after 60 delivered, individually reviewed messages: change niche or offer before sending more.
- Fewer than two paid clients after 10 qualified demonstrations: change pricing, proof, or implementation scope.
- Any client-specific platform cost that cannot be passed through: do not onboard that client under the founding price.
