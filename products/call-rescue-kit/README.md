# Call Rescue Kit

Call Rescue Kit is a productized implementation service for U.S. home-service businesses that lose inbound leads after hours or when calls are missed.

## Offer

- Missed-call text-back
- After-hours AI receptionist
- Lead qualification
- Appointment booking
- Daily lead summary
- 14-day launch and tuning support

## Pricing

- Founding client implementation: $1,000 one-time
- First month monitoring and tuning: $250
- Ongoing monitoring and tuning: $249/month
- Telephony and AI usage: billed separately at cost or provisioned in the client's account

Four founding-client packages produce $5,000 in first-month revenue.

## Initial $100 budget

- Domain: up to $15
- Vercel Pro: $20
- AI phone-agent testing: up to $30
- Domain email and deliverability: up to $20
- Contingency: at least $15

No paid advertising is authorized during initial validation.

## Operating boundary

The agent system may research public business information, build evidence-based audits, prepare personalized outreach, create demos, and generate implementation assets. Sending outreach, purchasing services, charging customers, changing customer systems, or publishing publicly requires explicit human approval.

## Deployment

This directory is a static Vercel-compatible site. Create a new Vercel project from the repository and set the root directory to `products/call-rescue-kit`.

## 30-day success gates

1. Landing page deployed and independently audited.
2. 50 qualified prospects with evidence of weak after-hours or missed-call handling.
3. 20 personalized outreach drafts.
4. 5 discovery calls booked.
5. 4 paid founding-client implementations.
